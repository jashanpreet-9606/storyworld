Best Selling Authors



Chetan Bhagat (born 22 April 1974) is an Indian author, columnist and youtuber. He was included in Time magazine's list of World's 100 Most Influential People in 2010.

Stephen Edwin King (born September 21, 1947) is an American author of horror, supernatural fiction, suspense, crime, science-fiction, and fantasy novels."

Joanne Rowling CH OBE FRSL ( born 31 July 1965), also known by her pen name J. K. Rowling, is a British author and philanthropist. She wrote Harry Potter.

Shashi Tharoor, (born March 9, 1956, London, England), prominent Indian diplomat and politician. He is also a highly regarded author of both nonfiction and fiction books.

Paulo Coelho de Souza (born 24 August 1947) is a Brazilian lyricist and novelist and a member of the Brazilian Academy of Letters since 2002.

Rupi Kaur (born 4 October 1992) is a Canadian poet, illustrator, photographer, and author. Born in Punjab, India, Kaur emigrated to Canada at a young age.

Sudha Murty (born 19 August 1950), occasionally spelled in media as Sudha Murthy, is an Indian educator, author and philanthropist.

William Shakespeare ( 26 April1564 - 23 April 1616) was an English playwright, poet and actor. He is widely regarded as the greatest writer in the English.

Nora Roberts (born Eleanor Marie Robertson on October 10, 1950) is an American author of more than 225 romance novels. She writes as J. D. Robb for the in Death series .

Kazuo Ishiguro OBE FRSA FRSL(8 November 1954 (age 67 years) Nagasaki, Nagasaki, Japan) is an English novelist, screenwriter, musician, and short-story writer.
